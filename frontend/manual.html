1. System Overview

2. Executive Dashboard

The dashboard provides a real-time snapshot of business health using advanced analytics.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Revenue
Total gross income from sales in the selected period.
Net Profit
Revenue minus sold-item direct costs and rent for the selected period.
Sold Direct Costs
Calculated from sold quantities only: Sold Wire Cost + Sold Conversion Cost + Transport to Market.
Utilization (Efficiency)
Measures material wastage. Compares expected wire weight vs. actual wire used.
Profit Calculation Profit = Total Sales - (Purchase Costs + Operator Wages + Knuckler Wages + Sack Costs + Rent)

3. Daily Data Entry

The system prevents "data drift" by requiring consecutive entry. If yesterday's data is missing, today's entry is blocked for production staff.

📦 Purchases

Records incoming raw wire. The system automatically calculates the Landed Cost.

Landed Cost Formula Landed Cost per Kg = (Total Bought * Cost per Kg + Transport Cost) / Total Bought

⚙ Production

Tracks how raw material is converted into finished pieces. Integrity Control: You cannot use more wire than is available in stock.

Integrity Control

The system validates that Pieces Produced * Unit Weight does not exceed Total Wire Used. If wastage is too high, the Utilization KPI will drop below 90% (marked in Red).

💰 Sales

Records revenue. Integrity Control: You cannot sell pieces that haven't been produced (prevents negative stock).

4. Inventory & Stock Control

Automated stock tracking for both Raw Materials and Finished Goods.

Raw Stock
Total Bought - Total Used in Production. Alerts trigger when below 100kg.
Finished Goods
Total Produced - Total Sold. tracked per piece type (e.g., 2x2 Roll).
Stock Value
Current inventory multiplied by default selling prices.

5. Financial Reconciliation

A specialized module for System Owner to track what the business owes to production staff and suppliers.

⚖ Liability Pipeline

  • Production Staff Wages: Accrued automatically per piece produced during production entry.
  • Supplier Balances: Accrued when purchases are recorded but not yet marked as paid.
  • Rent Automation: The system detects a new month and automatically generates a rent liability based on your configuration.
Outstanding Liability Liability = (Accrued Wages + Accrued Supplier Bills + Rent Due) - Total Payments Made

6. System Administration

Tools for security, data portability, and configuration.

  • Audit Log: Every single change (Edit, Delete, Custom Price) is logged with the timestamp and the user who performed it.
  • Backup & Restore: Owners can download the entire database as a .db file. This file can be re-imported to restore the system to a previous state.
  • Global Config: Centralized control for wage rates, transport costs, and business branding.
